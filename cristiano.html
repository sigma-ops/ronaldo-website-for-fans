About Cristiano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo, often considered one of the greatest football players of all time, has captivated fans worldwide with his incredible skills, dedication, and unmatched work ethic. Born on February 5, 1985, in Madeira, Portugal, he has achieved numerous records and accolades during his illustrious career.

Career Highlights

  • 5-time Ballon d'Or winner
  • All-time leading scorer for Portugal and in the UEFA Champions League
  • Multiple league titles with Manchester United, Real Madrid, and Juventus
  • 2026 FIFA World Cup Golden Boot
